Rentals

Here is introducion of Rentals.

Soft surfboard rentals

  • Soft surfboards are generally much safer for the beginner surfer.
  • The boards are also extremely durable.
  • Some performance limitations

Funboard rentals

  • Ideal for learning to surf
  • Easily catches waves
  • Difficult to duck-dive
  • Not ideal for hollow or larger surf

Fish surfboard rentals

  • Performs well in weak/small surf
  • Thick boards like a fish don't snap as easily
  • Only suitable for intermediate+ surfers
  • Not suited for hollow or large waves

Boogie board rentals

  • small, more rectangular boards you lay on rather than standing
  • one of the safest options for families in small waves
