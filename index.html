Why ENJOY Surf Camp?

Professional

All of our instructors have been carefully chosen - meaning we are a cohesive, fun loving team. All instructors are certified in CPR & First Aid in accordance with BCASI regulations.

High quality of lessons

In our programs you will catch all the waves your body can stand while our expert coaches fill your mind with knowledge and your spirit with stoke. When you leave our program you will have an understanding of why surfers are surfers and why we have uncompromising commitments to coastal conservation.

TEACHING METHODOLOGY

  • Safety is our first priority. We never exceed a student-to-instructor ratio of 3:1.
  • Correct surfing technique
  • Appropriate surf etiquette
  • Support progress (at every level) in each student
  • Encourage ecological conservation

Come “sea life differently” and experience for yourself what everyone is talking about. You will have an adventure that will change your life forever!
